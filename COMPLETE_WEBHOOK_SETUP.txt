========================================
  COMPLETE STRIPE WEBHOOK SETUP
========================================

YOU ARE HERE: "Configure your destination" step

NEXT STEPS:
-----------

1. Click "Continue →" button (bottom right)

2. On next screen, you'll see:
   - "Choose destination type" 
   - Select "Webhook endpoint"

3. Enter your webhook URL:
   https://crownworksnl.com/api/webhook

4. Select events (or select all events)

5. Click "Add endpoint" or "Create"

6. After creation, you'll see:
   - Endpoint details
   - "Signing secret" section
   - Click "Reveal" to see the secret
   - Copy the secret (starts with whsec_)

7. Add to Vercel:
   - Go to: https://vercel.com/dashboard
   - Your project → Settings → Environment Variables
   - Add: STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET = whsec_...

========================================
  AFTER WEBHOOK IS CREATED
========================================

You'll also need your Stripe Secret Key:
- Go to: https://dashboard.stripe.com/apikeys
- Click "Reveal test key" or "Reveal live key"
- Copy the key (starts with sk_live_ or sk_test_)
- Add to Vercel as: STRIPE_SECRET_KEY
