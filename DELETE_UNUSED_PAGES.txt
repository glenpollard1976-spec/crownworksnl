========================================
  HOW TO DELETE UNUSED FACEBOOK PAGES
  Quick Steps
========================================

STEP 1: See All Your Pages
---------------------------
Go to: https://www.facebook.com/pages

You'll see a list of all pages you manage.

STEP 2: Identify Unused Pages
------------------------------
Look for:
- Old test pages
- Pages with 0 likes/followers
- Pages you forgot about
- Pages you never use
- Duplicate pages

STEP 3: Delete a Page
---------------------
For each unused page:

1. Click on the page name
2. Click "Settings" (left sidebar)
3. Click "General" (in Settings)
4. Scroll to bottom
5. Find "Remove Page" or "Delete Page"
6. Click it
7. Confirm deletion

STEP 4: Repeat
--------------
Delete 2-3 unused pages to free up space.

STEP 5: Create CrownWorksNL Page
---------------------------------
After deleting:
1. Go to: https://www.facebook.com/pages/create
2. Create "CrownWorksNL" page
3. Should work now!

========================================
  TIP: You can always recreate deleted
  pages later if you need them.
========================================
