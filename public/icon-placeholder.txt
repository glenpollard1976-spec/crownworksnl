ICON PLACEHOLDER - Replace with actual icons

You need to create two icon files:
1. icon-192.png (192x192 pixels)
2. icon-512.png (512x512 pixels)

You can:
- Use a design tool like Canva, Figma, or Photoshop
- Use an online icon generator
- Hire a designer

The icons should:
- Represent CrownWorksNL brand
- Be clear and recognizable at small sizes
- Use the brand colors (indigo #4f46e5)
- Be square with transparent or solid background

Once created, place them in the /public folder:
- public/icon-192.png
- public/icon-512.png
