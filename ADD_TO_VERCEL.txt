========================================
  ADD KEYS TO VERCEL - QUICK STEPS
========================================

YOU HAVE: Stripe Secret Key ✅

YOU NEED: Webhook Secret (from webhook endpoint)

========================================
  STEP 1: CREATE WEBHOOK (if not done)
========================================

1. Go to: https://dashboard.stripe.com/webhooks
2. Click "Add endpoint"
3. URL: https://crownworksnl.com/api/webhook
4. Select events (or select all)
5. Click "Add endpoint"
6. Click endpoint name
7. Click "Reveal" next to "Signing secret"
8. Copy the secret (starts with whsec_)

========================================
  STEP 2: ADD TO VERCEL
========================================

1. Go to: https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. Click your "crownworksnl" project
3. Click "Settings" tab
4. Click "Environment Variables" (left sidebar)

ADD VARIABLE 1:
- Name: STRIPE_SECRET_KEY
- Value: [Paste your Stripe secret key]
- Environment: ✅ Production
- Click "Save"

ADD VARIABLE 2:
- Name: STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET
- Value: [Paste your webhook secret]
- Environment: ✅ Production
- Click "Save"

ADD VARIABLE 3:
- Name: NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL
- Value: https://crownworksnl.com
- Environment: ✅ Production
- Click "Save"

========================================
  STEP 3: REDEPLOY
========================================

Vercel will auto-redeploy, or:
1. Go to "Deployments" tab
2. Click "..." on latest deployment
3. Click "Redeploy"

Wait 2-3 minutes, then test!
