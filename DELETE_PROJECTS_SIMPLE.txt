HOW TO DELETE VERCEL PROJECTS
==============================

STEP 1: Click on a project name in Vercel dashboard

STEP 2: Click "Settings" (gear icon, top right)

STEP 3: Scroll ALL the way down to bottom of page

STEP 4: Find "Danger Zone" section

STEP 5: Click red "Delete Project" button

STEP 6: Type the project name exactly (e.g., "crownworksnl")

STEP 7: Click "Delete" to confirm

STEP 8: Done! Repeat for next project.
