===================

1. Go to: https://vercel.com/websitenl

2. Click on project name (e.g., "crownworksnl")

3. Click "Settings" (top right)

4. Scroll to bottom

5. Click "Delete Project" (red button)

6. Type project name

7. Click "Delete"

8. Repeat for:
